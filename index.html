About

A Life Devoted to
the Perfect Roast

Kevin Ihachiz Livole is a specialty coffee professional with over eleven years of international experience spanning Kenya, Kuwait, Qatar, and Bahrain. His career is a study in mastery — moving from barista excellence to the precision science of roasting and quality control.

His expertise spans the full specialty coffee value chain: from green bean evaluation and roast profiling to sensory analysis, cupping protocol, and team development. He has led operations at some of the Gulf region's most respected specialty coffee brands.

Certified by the Specialty Coffee Association across Barista, Roasting, and Sensory disciplines, Kevin brings both academic rigour and hands-on craft to every engagement.
